Spinach Banchan

Spinach Banchan is a popular Korean side dish made of blanched and seasoned spinach.

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic clove, grinded
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 2 teaspoon seasame seeds, grinded
  • 8 oz spinach
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon of regular soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon seaseme oil

Steps

  1. Thoroughly wash the spinach
  2. Put a bowl of water to boil
  3. Blanch the spinach for 1 minute, until it shrinks in size
  4. Put the spinach in an ice bath
  5. Rinse and squeeze out the excess water
  6. Make the spinach into a small ball and cut into 4 pieces
  7. Mix the spinach, garlic, green onion, seasame seeds, soy sauce and seasame oil
  8. Transfer to a bowl and serve
