Hwachae

Hwachae is a refreshing Korean Watermelon Punch, which contains other fruits, and its served in a hollowed out watermelon

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Balled Watermelon
  • 1/2 Lime Soda
  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • 1/8 Honeydew
  • 1/8 Strawberry
  • 1/8 Banana
    • can also switch for other fruits of choice
  • Watermelon Bowl
  • 1 Cup of Ice

Steps

  1. Thoroughly wash all fruits and cut into small pieces
  2. Add balled watermellon and other fruits inside the watermelon bowl
  3. Pour the soda, milk and ice inside the watermelon bowl
  4. Stir until everything is combined and serve while cold
